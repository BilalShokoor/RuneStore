## 3. Price Premium Multipliers

These are the factors that increase account value above baseline:

| Factor | Premium | Evidence |
|--------|---------|----------|
| **Infernal Cape** | +$100 to +$200 | AGS Pure: $199 without vs $399 with (LCG) |
| **Dizana's Quiver** | +$20 to +$50 | Usually bundled with Infernal Cape |
| **Quest Cape (332 QP)** | +$50 to +$100 | Shifts account from "mid" to "end game" |
| **No Email Set** | +$20 to +$50 | Advertised as premium security feature |
| **Legacy Login (vs Jagex Launcher)** | +$10 to +$30 | Legacy = easier handoff, no mandatory MFA |
| **Pets** | +$50 to +$200 each | 18 pets = +$2,000+ on RSOrder listing |
| **Bank Value (GP/items)** | Scales linearly | 11B bank = massive price premium |
| **Achievement Diaries** | +$50 to +$100 | Full completion adds value |
| **83 Construction** | Standard expectation | Ornate Pool unlock (full stat restore) |
| **Minor Ban History** | **-25%** | LCG discounts banned accounts 25% off |
| **Full Void Set** | +$20 to +$40 | Important for certain builds |
| **Barrows Gloves** | Required baseline | Account without BG is worth significantly less |

## 4. Best-Selling Account Types (Ranked by Volume & Demand)

### Rank 1: Starter/Bot Accounts ($0.38 - $5.99) - HIGHEST VOLUME
- Thousands in stock at single sellers
- 60 base melee is the sweet spot (2,148 units at Empirers)
- Razor-thin margins per unit, massive volume

### Rank 2: 1 Def Pures ($50 - $200) - HIGHEST PLAYER DEMAND
- Most popular PvP build in the game
- 40 ATT / 99 STR is the standard base configuration
- Adding Range levels creates clear upsell tiers
- Multiple sellers stock 4-13 units each
- LuckyCharmGold alone has 15+ pure variants

### Rank 3: Zerker Accounts ($49 - $499) - STRONG NICHE
- Second most popular PvP build
- Deep progression path: starter ($49) to Infernal ($499)
- LuckyCharmGold has 19 zerker variants - the deepest inventory
- Infernal Cape zerkers are the top-end at $399-$499

### Rank 4: Quest Cape End-Game Mains ($329 - $600) - STEADY DEMAND
- RSOrder stocks 9+ Quest Cape variants
- Average price ~$370 makes this a healthy mid-market segment
- Less competition than the pure space

### Rank 5: Ironman/HCIM ($200 - $2,000+) - GROWING NICHE
- Highest margins due to production difficulty
- Supply-constrained (much longer to train)
- HCIM with no deaths is the holy grail

### Rank 6: Maxed Accounts ($300 - $10,000) - PREMIUM SEGMENT
- Low volume but massive per-unit margins
- Rare items and pets are the main differentiators at the top end
- PlayerAuctions sees listings up to $30,000
